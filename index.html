Echoes of the Underground

Some things never vanish — they just echo in the void

Digital traces are not easily erased... Some stories are buried, but they whisper to those who know where to listen

In this mission, you must follow the thin threads left by one of the most prominent figures in the data leak scene. He ran one of the world's largest websites, where the data of millions of users was being sold and exposed, until something unexpected happened.

Your task is to find out the name of the song he posted on his personal account on his website, because according to the information we have received, the song wasn't just a song, but rather a hidden message. [The Flag is the song name]

"When the light goes out, only echoes guide the seekers."

Seek not where the light shines, but where it once did. Search the tomb of the vanished domain.
